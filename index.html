The Journey

The Mill.

  • 2020-2021

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We are a team of three college kids who always had creative visuals and ideas. While we loved everything to do with story-telling, creative ads and trends, we decided to do lauch "The Mill" as our creative outlet.

  • March 2021

    An Agency is Born

    What started as a creative project became our burning passion. We did a few campaigns for small brands and those results gave us the courage and motivation to do it full-time!

  • December 2021

    Transition to Full Service

    After months of discussion and visualisation, we started pitching to a bunch of clients and started aiming for the stars. We were given our first break by the health-tech giant Fit-Bit and The Mill started spinning!

  • July 2023

    Phase Two Expansion

    After Fit-Bit's marketing sucess, we partnered with multiple fortune 500 companies and generated customer acquisiton and retention like nobody else! The Mill earned its spot!
